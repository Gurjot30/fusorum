What Is Accounting in a Billing ERP?

In traditional setups, businesses manage billing and accounting separately—causing data mismatches, delays in reporting, and compliance risks. With Fusorum, accounting is not an add-on. It’s an integrated ERP module that captures:

  • Every sale from your POS
  • Every invoice you send
  • Every expense or purchase
  • Every payment and tax detail
  • → Automatically posted to the right ledgers
  • → Synced in real-time across all branches and departments
  • → Always ready for reports, audits, or tax filing

What You Get with Fusorum ERP Accounting

  • 🧮 Auto-Sync With Billing & POS
    All invoices, sales, returns, and payments instantly reflected in your books.
  • 📑 Chart of Accounts & Journals
    Pre-configured ledgers or custom account setup for all financial activities.
  • 📤 Tax Reporting & Compliance
    Generate GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, TDS, credit notes, and audit trails effortlessly.
  • 📈 Financial Statements
    Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, Expense Breakdown.
  • 🔄 Bank Reconciliation
    Match your bank statement with in-system transactions.
  • 🔔 Alerts & Reminder
    Overdue invoices, low cash flow, and upcoming tax due dates.
  • 🧾 Multi-Branch Accounting
    Track and manage books separately or consolidated across branches.
