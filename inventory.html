What Is Inventory Management in an ERP System?

Inventory Management in a billing ERP refers to the centralized tracking and control of stock—linked directly to sales, purchases, returns, and billing activities. Fusorum’s inventory system updates stock in real time as soon as:

  • A sale is made (POS or invoice)
  • Stock is purchased or received
  • Items are returned or exchanged
  • Adjustments or transfers are recorded

deal for Businesses That Depend on Stock Accuracy

  • 🏪 Retail Chains & Stores – Apparel, grocery, electronics
  • 🧴 FMCG Distributors – Multi-brand, multi-location inventory
  • 🏥 Pharmaceuticals – Batch and expiry control
  • 🍽️ Restaurants & Cafes – Raw material tracking and bundling
  • 🧰 Hardware & Industrial Suppliers – Large catalogs with variants
  • 📦 Warehouses & Traders – High-volume bulk stock monitoring
